# Tax-Efficiency Strategy Template — for Tax Consultants

Thank you for contributing your expertise. Each strategy you add here helps the
app's AI suggest **legal, ethical** tax efficiencies to taxpayers.

## How to fill this in
1. **Copy the blank block below** (everything from `## Strategy:` to `App form:`).
2. **Paste it at the bottom** of this file and **fill in each line**. Add as many
   strategies as you like — one block each.
3. Keep each line on ONE line (don't press Enter inside a field).
4. Send the file back. An administrator reviews every entry and **approves** it
   before it goes live — nothing you write reaches taxpayers automatically.

## Rules (please)
- **LEGAL strategies only** — statutory reliefs, credits, allowances, reductions
  and exemptions under the Income Tax Ordinance 2001 / current Finance Act.
- **No evasion, no grey areas, no fabricated expenses.** If it wouldn't survive an
  FBR audit, leave it out.
- **Cite the section** (e.g. `s.63`, `Part III, Second Schedule`) wherever you can.
- This is **general tax knowledge** — never include any individual taxpayer's name,
  CNIC, figures, or personal data.

### Field guide
- **Title** — short name of the strategy (required).
- **Who / when** — the taxpayer profile or situation it applies to.
- **Relief** — the credit / allowance / reduction it uses.
- **Section** — the governing Ordinance section / schedule.
- **Cap** — any statutory limit (e.g. "20% of taxable income").
- **How** — what the taxpayer does / enters to claim it.
- **Caveat** — eligibility conditions or warnings.
- **App form** — which form in the app, exactly one of:
  `deductions`, `credits`, `reductions`, `income`, `adjustable-tax`,
  `final-min-income`, `capital-gains` (or leave blank).

## Examples (you can edit these or delete them)

## Strategy: Voluntary pension (VPS) contribution tax credit
Who / when: Salaried individuals who can contribute to an approved pension fund
Relief: Tax credit for contribution to an approved pension fund
Section: s.63
Cap: 20% of taxable income
How: Contribute to an SECP-approved pension fund and enter it in the Credits form
Caveat: Must be an SECP-approved VPS fund; confirm eligibility and any age-based extra
App form: credits

## Strategy: Charitable donations tax credit
Who / when: Anyone who donated to an approved / listed charitable institution
Relief: Tax credit for charitable donations
Section: s.61
Cap: 30% of taxable income (15% if the donee is an associate)
How: Enter the donation in the Credits form; keep receipts
Caveat: Donee must be eligible/approved; cash donations above the limit may be excluded
App form: credits
